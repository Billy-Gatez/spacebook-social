MOONCRAFT: Beta Reference Guide

Overview

MOONCRAFT is a browser-based 3D survival game set on the lunar surface. You begin stranded near a damaged landing pod with a cracked suit and rapidly draining oxygen. Hostile alien creatures roam the terrain. Your mission is to mine resources, craft equipment, build shelters, and survive as long as possible. The entire game runs from a single HTML file in any modern browser.

System Requirements

  • Chrome 120+, Edge 120+, or Firefox 122+
  • No installation or server required after initial load
  • Three.js loads once from CDN and is cached
  • Safari not recommended (pointer lock issues)

Getting Started

  1. Visit provided link at https://spacebook-app.onrender.com/view?page=betamooncraft
  2. Open the file in a supported browser
  3. Select a world slot and click Play or New
  4. Click the game canvas to activate mouse look

World & Terrain

Each world is procedurally generated with regolith plains, basalt ridges, craters, ice deposits, and ore veins. A landing pad made of Plating marks the spawn point at world origin.

Alien Enemies

Aliens spawn continuously in Survival Mode. They pursue the player, deal melee damage, and fire red projectiles from range.

  • Pursue within ~20 blocks
  • Deal melee damage up close
  • Fire projectiles from 3–16 blocks
  • Require 4 Space Ray hits to defeat
  • Drop oxygen and occasional ore

Alien Swarm: Spawns four additional aliens at once.

Space Ray

Your primary weapon. Fires automatically when left-clicking in open air or manually with R. Projectiles deal damage and knockback. Aliens drop oxygen when defeated.

World Saves

  • Three save slots available
  • Slots show last save date and mode
  • Save with Ctrl+S or pause menu
  • Saves stored in localStorage

Creative Mode

  • No damage
  • Infinite oxygen
  • Unlimited blocks
  • All recipes unlocked
  • No alien spawns

Survival Tips

  • Mine Basalt early and build a perimeter wall
  • Stay mobile to avoid alien swarms
  • Kill aliens regularly to maintain oxygen
  • Save often | no autosave
  • Anomaly ore is extremely rare
  • The Space Ray outranges alien projectiles
